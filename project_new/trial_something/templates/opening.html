Our Goal

Self-advocacy: Knowledge is Power

For patients to be able to be their own advocate and have easy access to public information about the safety of the drug.  

For many drugs, clinical trial studies are not always representative of the general population. For instance, sometimes there are significantly less female participants, which ends up masking the true effect of the drug post-market.  

unbIAsed.Rx offers a solution for individuals to raise awareness of this issue, as well as provide the tools to better advocate for themselves.  


Impact

More Equitable and Effective Healthcare for All

  • For patients to look up specific drugs, to make more informed decisions about the drugs they take.  

  • For the healthcare system, to make more informed decisions about the drugs they prescribe.  

  • For the pharmaceutical industry, to raise awareness about the importance of diversity in clinical trials.  

  • For society as a whole, to raise awareness on the importance of representation and self-advocacy.
