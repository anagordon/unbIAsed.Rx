Our Goal

Self-advocacy: Knowledge is Power

For patients to become more active participants in their health-related decisions by providing easy and comprehensive access to public drug safety information (e.g.: number of clinical trials and the sex distribution of their participants, common side effects, etc.).  

Historically, drugs have been tested on demographics not always representative of the general population, leading to post-market adverse effects disproportionately affecting underrepresented groups.  

unbIAsed.Rx addresses this concern by raising awareness about the imbalances in clinical trials, equipping individuals with the tools they need to effectively advocate for themselves and their health.  


Impact

More Equitable and Effective Healthcare for All

  • For patients to make informed decisions about their medication by exploring relevant drug information and discussing it with their healthcare practitioners.  

  • For healthcare practitioners to review testing conditions beforehand for tailored medication prescriptions.  

  • For the pharmaceutical industry and the society as a whole to raise awareness about diversity matters in healthcare research and clinical trials, and to promote self-advocacy for one’s health.
